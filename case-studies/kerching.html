How a scientifically structured UX/UI solution transformed a failing platform after three unsuccessful redesigns

Client Background

Kerching

Industry: Ecommerce – Discount Coupons

Kerching is a digital platform offering discount coupons to help users save money while shopping online. The company had previously re-designed their website multiple times, but struggled to achieve results due to poor user experience.

Platform Focus

Digital discount coupons for online savings

The Challenge

Multiple failed redesigns and key issues

The Challenge

Despite multiple redesigns, the website still faced key issues:

  • Poor User Experience: Visitors struggled to navigate, leading to frustration
  • Low Engagement: Users weren't spending time on the site or converting
  • High Bounce Rates: Over 85% of users were leaving due to design flaws
  • Disconnect: The site lacked logic, flow, and emotional connection with users

Kerching needed a scientifically structured UX/UI solution that not only looked appealing but also improved functionality, engagement, and ultimately sales.
